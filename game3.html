Chess!

How to play Chess...

Each player has 16 pieces and the objective is to Checkmate the opponent's king.

Each piece moves differently (e.g., pawns forward, knights in an "L" shape) and players can Take opponent's pieces by landing on their square.

If you opponents King is in check with no escape; you win.
