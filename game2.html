How to play Snakes and Ladders

Two Teams compete to try and reach 100 on the board, sounds simple right? Guess again!

  • Players must account for the title of the game, the snakes and the ladders!
  • If a player lands on the foot of a ladder, they will climb up it skipping parts of the board!
  • Players must be wary of the snakes, if a player lands on the head of a snake, they will slide down its back, simultaniously losing their position on the board.
