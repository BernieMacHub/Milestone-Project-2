Noughts & Crosses

Player X: 0
Player O: 0
Draw: 0

How to Play

Two players take turn marking the board with either X or O markers in an attempt to line up 3 markers of your own horizonally, vertically and diagonally.

  • The player who lines up the first 3 markers wins the round, players can restart the game and tally the scores for multiple rounds.
  • If all grids on the board are filled with neither player managing to line up 3 markers, the game will result in a draw and also be added to the tally.
