Noughts & Crosses

How to Play Noughts and Crosses

Two players take turn marking the board with either X or O markers in an attempt to line up 3 markers of your own horizonally, vertically and diagonally.

  • The player who succeeds in placing three respective marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game
  • When all nine squares are full, the game is over. If no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a draw
  • The game will restart automatically after each game
